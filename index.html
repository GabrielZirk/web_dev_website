Whisper App

Visit (Repo)

A web app where people can publish their secrets anonymously. Sign up and tell us your deepest ones. 😊 The anonymously posted secrets can be viewed without registration and login.

Features:

  • Authentificaton: Passwords are salted and hashed.
  • User can delete their own secrets.
  • Admin privileges to delete any posted secret.
  • Password validation upon registration.

Frameworks/libraries used:

  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • passport.js
  • MongoDB
  • Mongoose
  • EJS
  • md5
